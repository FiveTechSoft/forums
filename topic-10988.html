Visual-Tools Version 1.1b ( 04.05.2008 )

Posted: Fri May 02, 2008 08:58 PM
Hello,

here is the 1. release of the visual-tools.

Everything, you will find in the tools, is 100 % existing FWH-code
without extra dll's or classes.

There are still some functions to be added like
previews of transparent images ( will be in FWH-release 8.05 )
The source creator for xbrowse and some other small
extensions.
But it is allready helpfull, to check backgrounds and color-combinations.
To ckeck Font-types and size in xbrowse and dialog-titles.
Everything works at runtime. The effect is shown directly,
as soon there are any changes.
The design of the Window-/ Dialog background and title-part can
be saved as a PRG-file with the needed functions.

Maybe somebody will have some ideas, to add more functions.
Color- and brush-handling of other FWH-parts could be included
as well.
To create nice color-combinations without preview, is not easy.
