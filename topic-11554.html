The people on this forum come from a Clipper background ( I've used it since the very first release, and my dbase experience dates to dBase II under CP/M ), and have all used FWH successfully to make Clipper programs into Windows programs.

Updates now come out every month, and they have many new features, plus fine tuning of existing elements. With FWH you can have your programs running with a user interface that is like the latest Vista programs ( or any other Windows style ), and in some cases you can get results that you can't get with normal Windows development tools.

In addition, you can retain your .DBF structure, or work with others, and you can continue to use .NTX, or perhaps you would like the performance of .CDX.

There are a lot of samples to work from, and people here really do help with any questions you post. Its a good place to get help.

Finally, on many other "forums" where people gather, I often see a lot of opinions on the software, and that leads no where. This forum focuses on helping people get the results they need, and on resolving problems that might arise. That means every post is usually valuable.

I hope this helps.
