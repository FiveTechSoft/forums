Perry,

Please link Harbour hbdebug.lib before FiveH.lib. In example, modifying FWH\samples\buildh.bat:

echo %hdirl%\hbdebug.lib %fwh%\lib\FiveH.lib %fwh%\lib\FiveHC.lib + >> b32.bc
