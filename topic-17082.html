Max,

From FWH 9.05 on you can use:

  1. oGrid1:Cols = 27
  2. oGrid1:Font:Name = "Arial"
  3. oGrid1:Column(2):Width = 20
  4. oGrid1:Cell(0,1):Text = "Item Code"
