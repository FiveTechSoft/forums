Thanks to superb help from Lailton (big thank you Master), now mod_harbour CGI supports all these URL formats:



http://localhost/test

http://localhost/test.prg

http://localhost/test?name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more" rel="noopener">http://localhost/test?name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more

http://localhost/test.prg?name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more" rel="noopener">http://localhost/test.prg?name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more

http://localhost/cgi-bin/modharbour.exe?test.prg

http://localhost/cgi-bin/modharbour.exe?prg=test.prg&name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more" rel="noopener">http://localhost/cgi-bin/modharbour.exe?prg=test.prg&name=fivetech&another=yes&andmuch=more



Simply copy modharbour.exe and DLLs from here to your cgi-bin folder:

https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/tree/master/cgi/windows" rel="noopener">https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/tree/master/cgi/windows



Copy .hcaccess to apache/htdocs:

https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/blob/master/cgi/windows/.htaccess" rel="noopener">https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/blob/master/cgi/windows/.htaccess



And use this Apache configuration file:

https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/blob/master/cgi/windows/httpd.conf" rel="noopener">https://github.com/FiveTechSoft/mod_harbour/blob/master/cgi/windows/httpd.conf



and enjoy the power and robustness of mod_harbour CGI. Rock solid! :-) Watch the video bellow:
