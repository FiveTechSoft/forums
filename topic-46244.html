FiveWiDi, I think this is less a concrete bug in Hix and more a design issue.

Tasks that may take a long time or can potentially end up in infinite loops should be offloaded to separate workers or processes, not executed in the server’s main thread.

The server should act as an orchestrator rather than as the execution environment for long-running jobs, in order to avoid blocking, memory issues, or complete service crashes.
