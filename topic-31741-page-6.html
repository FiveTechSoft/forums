Günther,

You are using a wrong manifest file.

Do it this way: Remember to provide -D__64__ to the resources compiler for 64 bits

ifndef 64

1 24 "WinXP/WindowsXP.Manifest"

else

1 24 "WinXP/WindowsXP.Manifest64"

endif
