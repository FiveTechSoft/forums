For the most part Windows11 runs well however I have two pet peeves ..

1) Right Click on any file or icon .. in W10 you have to option to Delete, copy, send to, Etc, in Windows 11 ... you have a "cute" menu with a Trash can in the right corner .. but if you want the old W10 menu .. you have to go to the bottom and click on "more option" to see all your options like Windows 10 had in one click

2) Open several folders .. on the task bar you see the folder with a little dot which represents you have several "tiled" folders open .. where as W10 all your folders open as separate boxes on the task bar ... the Win 11 tiled option gets too confusing and I have found myself closing the wrong folder by accident ..

Otherwise, Windows 11 seems to compile all FW 32 bit programs as in 10 and run quite will .. especially ADO works just like it did in 10, maybe a little faster ...
