by adiant » Fri Mar 13, 2009 12:56 am

Common word "filtering" can also be disabled in the ACP (admin panel) in Search Settings:
Common word threshold:
Words which are contained in a greater percentage of all posts will be regarded as common. Common words are ignored in search queries. Set to zero to disable. Only takes effect if there are more than 100 posts. If you want words that are currently regarded as common to be reconsidered you have to recreate the index.adiant
