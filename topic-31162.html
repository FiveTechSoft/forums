Hello,

Here are some of the tools that I use at the moment.

-Editor: gVim (http://www.vim.org/) (because the color high lighting works well, in notepad++ I could not get this working well)
-Searching files: Total Commander (http://www.ghisler.com/)
-dbf tools: Libre office Calc (https://www.libreoffice.org/discover/calc/) , DBF Viewer 2000 Free Trial (http://www.dbf2002.com/)
-SQL tool: Heidi SQL (http://www.heidisql.com/)
-SQL database server: Mysql (http://www.mysql.com/downloads/)
-Clipper Compiler: Xharbour123 with C compiler: Borland 5.82
-Library: Fivewin 2015
