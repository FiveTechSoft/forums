Welcome at IT-Diet!
Losing weight should make easier your life…
That’s why we have developed an optimal support. SMS-Diet-Control is not complicated and easy to handle, all you need is a computer and a cell-phone.

Motivation-SMS
Our special “Motivation-SMS”-Feature helps you to resist in critical situations.

We give you the opportunity – seize it!

We can’t give you a guarantee for losing weight. IT-diet helps and supports you in the difficult fight against your pounds!

The idea – the system – the draft

The idea
The idea of the sms-method is to rate your habit of eating with school rates.

Registration of the data
You rate yourself directly on the spot – after eating you send a sms to it-diet.

The system is persistent
Everyday at the same time the system reminds you to send your assessment of your eating habits.

Statistics
On the internet you can have a look at your automatically drawn up statistics.

Condition
After some time follows a condition of cell phone or email with it-diet

Unerring
The system is unerring. Once you have given your data you can’t change them anymore.

How to create a sms
After every meal send a sms with your mark (1-5) to it-diet.

  1. password spacing

  2. sign
    G Weight
    F Breakfast
    M Lunch
    A Meal
    J Snack
    B Sports
    ? Question

  3. after the sign without a space your rating (1-5)/your weight.
    Password m2
    f.e.: for lunch you rate yourself with a 2
