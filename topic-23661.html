Lucas,

Puedes compilar cada uno de los ficheros C:

bcc32.exe -c -Ic:\harbour\include -Ic:\acesdk\acesdk ads1.c
bcc32.exe -c -Ic:\harbour\include -Ic:\acesdk\acesdk adsfunc.c
bcc32.exe -c -Ic:\harbour\include -Ic:\acesdk\acesdk adsmgmnt.c
bcc32.exe -c -Ic:\harbour\include -Ic:\acesdk\acesdk adsx.c

y luego construir la libreria usando tlib.exe:

tlib.exe adsrdd.lib -+ ads1.obj adsfunc.obj adsmgmnt.obj adsx.obj
