SET MESSAGE OF oWnd TO "Pantalla Principal del Sistema ................" NOINSET CLOCK DATE KEYBOARD

@ 2,1 IMAGE oBmp FILENAME "IMAGEN2.jpg" NOBORDER SIZE 1260,865 OF oWnd SCROOL ADJUST
oBmp:lTransparent = .T.
