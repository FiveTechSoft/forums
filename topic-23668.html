Hi Lucas,

1. Issue cAlias->( AdsCacheRecords( <nRecs> ) ) or TABLE->( AdsCacheRecords( 50 ) ).. in my case, it improves the speed.
2. on your ads.ini:

[Settings]
COMPRESSION = Always
USE_TCP_IP = 1
PACKET_SIZE = 512 ;or 1024

This also improves my speed.
