Hi Oscar.
Try this:

In your main.prg: "Include "XBrowse.ch"
Replace this REDEFINE LISTBOX oLbx, with REDEFINE XBROWSE oLbx
Remove all oLbx:UpStable()
Replace this CONTROL "", 8000, "twbrowse", with CONTROL "", 8000, "txbrowse" ( in your .rc)
