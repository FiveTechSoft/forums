Fivewin app on android and iphone
Posted: Mon Apr 25, 2011 06:35 PM
Hi all,
I have a lot of customers that requiring me a solution to use my FWH accounting app also on iPhone and Android.
Unfortunately developing a FWH app for iPhone is expensive and for Android isn't possibile - as I know,
furthermore a vpn connection beetween the mobile phone and the server is also often unstable.

I have found this really interesting solution based on TeamViewer I want to share with you.
- the customer has to install TeamViewer for Windows on the server and the Teamviewer for iPhone/Android on the mobile. TeamViewer can be downloaded from http://www.teamviewer.com and it is totally free for private use.
- the customer connect the mobile to the server. When my accounting app start I ask to the customer if it wants to access in Desktop mode (default) or Mobile mode. Selecting Mobile mode I use a special developed interface optimized for an 800x400 resolution.

This is the result (on Acer liquid with Android).
