Hi everyone;

Sergey Spirin's passing away is a very sad event. This was a great man and developer.

Since Sergey now rests in peace, I don't know how much longer the frh google group will be maintained. Being that Frax_Frh is used by a lot of fwinners, perhaps we can all pull together to help each other with questions about frh. I still think that even though Sergey is no longer around, FastReports is the best report engine available to the xbase world BY FAR and nothing even comes close.

I've been using frh since last Nov (2011) and feel like I understand it pretty well. So I offer my help to anyone posting questions on this forum. I know there are other fwinners who are quite good at frh also. I hope that this forum administrators won't mind.

On a similar subject; I'm thinking that Antonio would be an excellent candidate to take over FRH development. FastReports for xBase and Harbour was developed and sold by Sergey under a special license from the Fast Reports creators. I'm sure Antonio can take over and shouldn't have much resistance from FastReports to become the new librarian for FRAX_FRH. I sure hope Antonio is willing to try.
