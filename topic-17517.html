FYI, I just wanted to thank you Antonio for all your hard work, as well as all the other programmers that have worked on the FWH binaries, and the Harbour and xHarbour compilers. I'm very impressed with the results of the conversion from Clipper to Harbour and my customers are tickled pink with the ability to run the software on 64 bit platforms. I'm extremely pleased with the overall performance and the rock solid reliability of what I've seen thus far.

FYI, I'm currently running on the Harbour/Borland compiler platform but I'm looking at what xHarbour has to offer in terms of XML support and other object/language extensions. Any comments on experiences with xHarbour are welcome.

Antonio, I know you must catch a lot of heat when things don't work right, and there will always be new things that people want you to add to Fivewin, but I just wanted to let you know that there are a lot of us who've earned a good living using your products over the years who are very grateful for all your hard work. Thank you.
