Yo tambien uso una mezcla gdrive + usb + HD-SD + copias en el propio ordenador en varios HD debido a que la mayoria de las veces los problemas que obligan a restaurar copias no tienen que ver con ataques ransom ni virus, en mi caso al menos, tienen que ver con un del . y cosas asi o necesidad de volver a una version anterior porque hice unos cambios que quiero deshacer. To All



I don't mean to upset the apple cart here ... I am working in a full service computer store ... we have a sign that says :



THERE IS NO CLOUD ... JUST SOMEONE ELSE'S COMPUTER .... let me explain, this is how I explain this concept to a Customer.



The cloud is basically a datacenter somewhere and there are people ( employees ) that manage all that data and have access to all that personal information simply definable as "Someone else's computer" .. Think about that for a minute.... so let me ask you "Would you put your UserID and Password to your bank account on "Someone else's Computer ?"



Simple private solution .. Keep your code or personal data\information on a thumb drive on a lanyard around your neck .. If you still want to store a Customers data "on the Cloud" .. make sure all that data is encrypted !! ... my advice here comes from my personal experience as the IT Manager for the Criminal Justice Academy .. and believe me .. I have seen it ALL!!
