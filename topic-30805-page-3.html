Miracast is part of Win 8.1 but also present in Win 10.

A Miracast adapter is available from Microsoft that plugs into an HDMI and USB port ( both needed ) on a monitor or TV. I believe the USB is for power so perhaps an external power source will do the job.

Miracast is also available on ROKU models ( if you have that ) and the Amazon Firestick.

Most PC's, laptops, and tablets can use Miracast to share their video on the big screen. Some phones, both Windows and Android have this ability also.
