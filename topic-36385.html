Please use capitals for field datatypes, like "N","C","L","D","T","M".
Do not use "n","c","l","d","t"

In some cases Upper and Lower cases have different significance.

"C" : Creates VarChar
"c" : Creates Binary char field
"M" : Creates Long Text Field
"m" : Creates Long BLOB
