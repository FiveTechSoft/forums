New xbase publication on Medium

Posted: Fri Jan 13, 2017 06:40 PM
Hello,

For those who don't know it, Medium is a web publishing platform that allows both the publication of texts on a personal level and the creation of publications that are groups of texts by different authors with a common theme. Medium is oriented to the publication of original texts with a more elaborated content than other platforms, trying to be an alternative with texts of higher quality than other means.

I propose to all members of the forum the creation of a publication in Medium whose theme will be xbase programming. For example, articles such as the following may be published:

* Description of features or libraries of Harbour or xHarbour. Does anyone know hbformat() in depth?
* Description of features of the several GUI for [x]harbour.
* Description of features, code samples of libraries or functions that we have created or use, such as HDO or haruDPF to put the lastest ones that have appeared.
* Description of functionality, features or tutorials of programming tools: code editors, IDE, resource editors, etc. Cristobal could teach us all how FivEdit works.
* Description of functionalities of our programs that can serve as an example for other programmers, explaining the reason for the novelty of the functionality and how to implement it.
* Programming books or topics related to programming: psychology, sociology, work organization, productivity, etc.
* Any other topic related to programming in xbase languages.
* Experiences with other languages ​​/ programming environments.

Writing in Medium is simple and enjoyable. You can enter with a gmail account, facebook or twitter without registering. You can known more about Medium on ther Help Center: https://help.medium.com

I encourage you all to participate in the publication, I am sure that many of you can take some time out to write an article for the publication. I know that one of the things that slow down when creating and maintaining a blog is the commitment to publish frequently, but with a publication in Medium we can have something alive among several by publishing one article each every several weeks.

I sincerely believe that in this forum there are many people with great things to tell and share with the others. We can do a great job of spreading our favorite programming environment. A nice way to contribute to the xbase community.

Interested parties please write to joseluis@alanit.com

Many thanks to all for your attention.
