DOC-BASED APPOINTMENT System

I have programmed a simple document-based appointment system using TotalCommander.

The program creates daily folders for the next years in advance.

Documents that you want to put on appointment are simply dragged into the appropriate folder.

The program shows all day folders and marks folders with content. You can then open TotalCommander directly from the day.

On the left you will see all day folders and on the right the day is open.

The documents can then be easily edited, deleted and moved.
