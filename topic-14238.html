Uwe,

If you call oBmp:GetDC() then you have to make a call to oBmp:ReleaseDC() too:

hDC := oBmp1:GETDC(), ( ABPaint( hDC, 50, 50, oBmp1:hBitmap, 220 ), oBmp1:ReleaseDC() ), ...
