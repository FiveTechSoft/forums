How to use FW_GetExeName()
Posted: Fri May 09, 2026 10:14 am

Hello everyone,

FW_GetExeName() returns the full path of the running executable. Example:

local cExe := FW_GetExeName()
? cExe  // C:\fwh\samples\test.exe

Use it when you need to locate resources next to your EXE. Works on both Harbour and xHarbour builds.
