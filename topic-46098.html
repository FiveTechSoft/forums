Here’s a concise **How to Play** for Space-Invaders-style game:

# Goal

Defend your ship and destroy all aliens before they reach the bottom. Rack up points and survive as many levels as possible.

# Controls

  • Left / Right Arrow* – Move

  • * **Space** – Fire
  • * **P** – Pause/Resume
  • * **Start** – Begin a new game
  • * **Reset** – Reset the current game

    • # Scoring

  • Alien A (bottom rows): 10 pts*

  • * **Alien B (middle rows)**: **20 pts**
  • * **Alien C (top row)**: **30 pts**
  • * **UFO** (crosses the top): **50–200 pts** (random)

    • # Lives & Hits

  • You start with 3 lives*.

  • * Getting hit by an alien bullet costs **1 life**.
  • * At **0 lives**: **Game Over**.

    • # Levels & Difficulty

  • Clear all aliens to advance to the next level*.

  • * Each level increases alien **speed** and **fire rate**.

    • # UFO

  • * Spawns at random intervals at the top and flies across the screen.

  • * Shoot it for a **bonus**.

    • # Tips

  • Fire with intent:* shots are faster to reload if the lane is clear.

  • * **Open a column:** clear one column to shoot the top rows more easily.
  • * **Lead the UFO:** aim slightly ahead of its path.
  • * **Center up:** don’t camp on the edges—aliens step down on direction change.

  • UFO* – Bonus “mystery ship” that crosses the top of the screen.

  • * **pts** – Points (score units).
  • * **HUD** – Head-Up Display (bar showing score/lives/level).
