Jeff,

If you need an inmediate conversion of your FWH Windows app to Mac, then I strongly suggest you WinBottler as the way to go:

http://winebottler.kronenberg.org/ It is a free product and works really fine

FiveMac lets you create native Mac OSX applications but you can not take a FWH app and recompile it as it is, as it is not fully compatible neither as mature as FWH. FiveMac for now is fine for new Mac OSX developments.
