Willy,

> It keeps impossible to select a zone by just clicking in it.

Its easy but little tricky

You have two bitmaps, one with the set picture and another with only colors, one different color for each area. This second one is hidden.

When the user clicks on the picture, you know the row and col of the mouse, and do a GetPixel( oBmp2:GetDC(), nRow, nCol ), oBmp2:ReleaseDC() on the second bitmap. Based on the color you get with GetPixel(), you know what area was selected.
