Hakan

I use MSVC 2012 , in order to generate a xp compatible exe you must have at your link options

/nologo /subsystem:WINDOWS,5.01 /force:multiple /NODEFAULTLIB:libc /nxcompat:NO /machine:X86 /Ignore:4006
