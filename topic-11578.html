Create these two files with an editor and put them into the c:\bcc55\bin directory. You may have to change the paths according to your setup.

bcc32.cfg
-I"c:\bcc55\include;c:\fwh\include;c:\xharbour\include"
-L"c:\bcc55\lib"

ilink32.cfg
-L"c:\bcc55\lib"
