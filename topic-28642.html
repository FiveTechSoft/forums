The University of Kassel takes leave of Mr. Stefan Haupt * 7 9 1961 † 14 4 2014 Mr. Stefan Haupt was as technical staff in field construction computer science and at the same time as an IT administrator in the Department of Bauin- engineer and environmental engineering at the University of Kassel worked.
