Hello everyone,
took some time but finally it's done!!

first of all, let me introduce myself... I'm Ricardo Arraes, a harbour developer since 2014, a full stack and mobile developer for some years and now, I'm proud to say that I'm a mod harbour enthusiast. I ve been excited with mod harbour since I discovered it through our community (and it's forums), so, now, I think it's my time to give back and help to spread the word

This course is a Mod Harbour and Mercury course, and it gives you all the information you need to start developing professional web applications and web services with mod harboud and, also, explains to you how to apply the MVC architecture to your project, in order to improve it's scalability and maintainbility.

*Don't worry, you don't really need to be a experienced web developer to start this course, because during the lessons I'll make sure you understand the basic concepts and I'll leave some orientations for you to expand your knowledge and continue your studying.

The course starts from the very beginning (installing components, configuring the environment...) and continues until we reach some advanced topics, religiously following the mercury manual.

The hotmart platform that I decided to use to launch the course is very interactive and easy to use.

Well, hope you enjoy the course and keep taking big steps into your developer carreer!

*If there's any doubts/questions about the course, please send a email to harbour.ricardo@gmail.com

**Excuse my english, I know it's not the BEST, but I think it is enough for us to understand each other
