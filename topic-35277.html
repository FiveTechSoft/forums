FWH Barcode Reader for Tablets
Posted: Fri Feb 16, 2018 10:13 PM

As we look at various possibilities for FWH extensions, I would like to suggest the incorporation of a Barcode reader control that works with laptop/notebook and tablet computers. There are readers available but they do not integrate with the keyboard.

A standard barcode device will read the code, and translate it to alphanumeric text, placing it in the active field.

What we need is the ability to use a device's built in camera to scan the code and input it into our active field in our FWH applications.

Barcodes are a huge time saving capability and using them in this way allows us to make more valuable programs for our clients.

There are some bluetooth readers available but they have mixed reliability. Most tablets do not have the ability to plug in a USB dongle to work with those readers. This would be a great solution.

Anyone like this idea ?
