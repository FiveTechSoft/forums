Silvio,

We use a simple BAT file that easier the emulator configuration:
c:\Program Files\Microsoft\Device Emulator Preview\PPC_2003_SE\go.bat

..\deviceemulator ppc_2003_se_wwe_armv4.bin /skin Pocketpc_2003_skin.xml /memsize 128 /sharedfolder c:\pocket

that way you don't need ActiveSync at all. Simply copy your EXEs to c:\pocket and they are accesible from the emulator "memory" card
