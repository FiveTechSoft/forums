OUCHHH SOLVED ...

Very simple at all ...

? "Hello word"
does nothing in HRB

whilst
AP_RPuts( "Hello word" )
Work fine

My mistake clearly as the testhrb.prg was written correctly before I rewrote it :/
Thinking about it I should have seen it immediatly because compiling with harbour.exe cannot change ? to AP_RPUTS() function ...
